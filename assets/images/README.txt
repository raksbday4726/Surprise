HOW TO ADD YOUR PHOTOS
──────────────────────
1. Copy your photo files into this folder (assets/images/)
   Supported: .jpg .jpeg .png .webp

2. Open story.html and update two arrays in the <script>:

   A) photoGallery[] — the Pinterest / Polaroid gallery section
      Change the src fields to match your filenames:
        src: "assets/images/photo1.jpg"

   B) memories[] — the scroll-through polaroid section
      Change the photo fields:
        photo: "assets/images/mem1.jpg"

3. You can use any filenames — just make sure they match exactly.

TIP: Use WebP format for faster loading on mobile.
