💌 a letter for you

To Raksitha,
with love

My dearest Raksitha,

I've been trying to find words big enough to hold everything I feel when I think of you. I don't think they exist — not in any language, not in any song, not in any universe.

On the day I first spoke to you, I had no idea what was beginning. I had no idea that the ordinary moments I'd lived my whole life were quietly making space for something extraordinary. You.

You are the kind of person who makes the world softer just by being in it. You make ordinary Tuesday evenings feel sacred. You make everything — everything — better.

I think about the day you first laughed with me, really laughed, and I remember thinking: this is it. this is the sound I want to keep.

So here we are. Another year around the sun for you, and I'm just so impossibly grateful that I get to be the person who knows you. Who got to watch you grow, shine, and become more yourself with every passing day.

Today is yours. Every star in the sky belongs to you. Happy Birthday, Raksitha. You are my favorite chapter.

Always yours, Rahul 💜
